Lebon Siu – Graphic Designer


Enter the Rabbit Hole

Hi! My name is Lebon Siu.
I’m a graphic designer based in Vancouver.


I design high-concept digital experiences for businesses, and bring a unique perspective to design through my background in film and video. I have had the privelage to work with clients such as Fairmont, BC Liquor, Vancouver is Awesome, and more.


Before I was a designer, I worked in music video production as a resident videographer and content creator. I’ve spent countless hours working with music video directors to help bring their stories to life, from designing their pitch decks, to filming on set, to editing their music videos.


Today, I bring my love of storytelling to design, and take influence in film, art, music and design. I believe in using the power of narrative to create emotional connections between business and consumer, and bring with me a multitude of technical skills that can elevate your projects above the rest.


Skills

HTML/CSS

With over four years of experience in front-end design, I use HTML/CSS to bring your businesses personality to the forefront, creating highly customized and immersive web experiences.

Editing & Motion Graphics

My background in videography means I'm proficient in video editing softwares such as Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, and Final Cut Pro. On my free time, you'll find me brushing up on my motion graphic skills.

Illustration

I am well-versed in all Adobe softwares such as XD, Photoshop and Illustrator, and often find myself combining my illustration skills with design.

Visual Builders & Platforms

I am a quick learner when it comes to learning new platforms. I have experience in online visual builders such as Shopify, Unbounce, Ceros, WordPress, and Flexitive. I'm also currently learning webflow.
